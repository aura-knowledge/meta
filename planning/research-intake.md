## 5. Gaps and Uncertainties

Data points that are weak, forward-dated, extrapolated, or need verification before publication.

| # | Topic | Issue | Suggested Action Before Publishing |
|---:|---|---|---|
| 1 | **National attention-span figure** | No direct measurement on Indian population; 47s/40s figures are global (Gloria Mark). Higher Indian screen time suggests reality may be worse, but this is extrapolation. | Flag as "global benchmark applied to India with caveat"; do not present as India-specific measurement. |
| 2 | **10-point IQ drop from multitasking** | Single study (Institute of Psychiatry, London); methodology debated; "temporary" qualifier often dropped in popular accounts. | Present with explicit caveat or omit from headline claims. |
| 3 | **Precise creator income figures** | Median ₹60,000–80,000/year and bottom-70% <₹2 lakh are self-reported / industry-estimated; informal transactions unreported. | Use as directional estimate, not exact national statistic. |
| 4 | **Gaming hours and market size** | 590M gamers, 13 hrs/week, $3.8B market from FICCI-EY; industry reports may inflate market potential. | Cross-check with Sensor Tower / DataReportal; label as industry estimate. |
| 5 | **Consumer surplus ($47B) and net economic drag** | ICRIER consumer-surplus methodology (Goolsbee-Klenow) has limitations; aggregating $351B + mental health + distraction risks double-counting. | Present cost and benefit channels separately; avoid a single "net loss" headline unless modeled carefully. |
| 6 | **Brain-change causality** | fMRI/EEG studies show correlation and structural differences in heavy short-form users; small samples; causality vs. self-selection debated. | Use "associated with" / "mirrors patterns seen in addiction," not "causes." |
| 7 | **Social-media detox effectiveness** | Conflicting RCTs: one found 50% reduction had no wellbeing effect; another found 1-week detox reduced depression 24.8%. | Present both; emphasize *problematic use patterns* over raw time. |
| 8 | **Platform moderation investment** | "Only 5% of Oversight Board cases from Central/South Asia" is a proxy for safety investment, not a direct budget figure. | Frame as accountability-proxy, not proof of budget allocation. |
| 9 | **Political deepfake exposure (75%)** | Survey-based; timing near 2024 election; may not reflect sustained baseline. | Cite as election-period finding, not everyday rate. |
| 10 | **Forward-dated regulations** | IT Rules 2026 amendments (3-hour takedown) and DPDP Act 2027 compliance deadlines are recent; verify current status at publication time. | Check latest MeitY / PIB notifications before finalizing. |
| 11 | **AI opportunity-cost data for India** | Research folder has little India-specific AI productivity or talent-demand data. | Supplement with NASSCOM, Stanford HAI AI Index 2025, OECD generative-AI reports. |
| 12 | **Intervention effectiveness** | Limited India-specific evidence on what reduces problematic use (digital minimalism, friction design, school curricula). | Flag as open question; cite global studies cautiously. |
