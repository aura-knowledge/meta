# Cross-Agent Review Prompt

Review this private draft before it becomes a public Aura Knowledge submission.

Check:

- privacy leaks
- whether the primary reader is specific
- whether the outcome is concrete
- whether the article scope is small enough
- whether claims are defensible
- whether sources are public
- whether the route should instead be org-feedback, article-erratum, source-challenge, or no public submission

Return:

- `blocking_findings`
- `non_blocking_findings`
- `recommended_route`
- `required_revisions`
