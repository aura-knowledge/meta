# Intake And Ideation

Use for rough Aura Knowledge article ideas, reusable findings, topic exploration, and early brainstorming.

## Process

1. Keep raw context private.
2. Ask only missing questions needed to identify:
   - primary reader
   - intended outcome
   - reader problem
   - smallest useful version
   - why this should be an article
3. Challenge weak assumptions, broad audiences, and oversized scope.
4. Use `../article-proposal-ideation/SKILL.md` for the detailed ideation checklist when the idea is still fuzzy.
5. Produce a route recommendation.

## Route Decision

- Article idea with clear reader/outcome -> `article-proposal`.
- Workflow, schema, ontology, or governance improvement -> `org-feedback`.
- Correction to existing article -> `article-erratum`.
- Problem with a source/reference -> `source-challenge`.
- Private, unsafe, or unclear -> keep private.

## Output

Return a concise purpose statement, smallest viable version, likely claims, source needs, privacy risks, abstraction examples, and route.
