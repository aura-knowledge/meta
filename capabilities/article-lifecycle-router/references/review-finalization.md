# Review And Finalization

Use for self-review, sibling-agent review, privacy checks, readiness decisions, and publication package preparation.

## Review Checklist

Check:

- primary reader is specific
- thesis is clear
- claims are defensible
- public sources support the claims
- scope is bounded
- privacy contract passes
- abstraction examples are present when private context influenced the work
- agent involvement is disclosed
- errata/source concerns are not being misfiled as new articles

## Risk Routing

- Tier 0-1 additive or mechanical work can proceed after checks.
- Tier 2 contract-changing work needs sibling-agent review.
- Tier 3 critical work needs human approval.

## Output

Return findings ordered by severity, readiness status, required fixes, optional follow-ups, and recommended lane.
